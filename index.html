DeepXray


We develop an algorithm that can detect 14 pathology from chest X-rays based on ChestXray14 dataset. In this work we proposed a new kind loss layer, which can significantly promote the performance of training deep networks for multi-label classification. We combine the new loss layer with auxiliary task to construct a deep convolution networks DeepXrayNet, which achieve state of -the art results on all 14 diseases. This is our paper
